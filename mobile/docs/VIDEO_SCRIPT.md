## Production Notes

### Suggested Background Music
- Upbeat, modern electronic/corporate style
- Volume at 20-30% under voiceover
- Crescendo at intro and outro

### Screen Recording Tips
1. Use a clean demo account with sample data
2. Record at 1080p or higher
3. Smooth, deliberate cursor movements
4. Pause briefly on important elements
5. Add subtle zoom effects on key features

### Text Overlays (Suggested)
- Feature names as headers when switching sections
- Key statistics/numbers highlighted
- Social links at the end

### Recommended AI Video Tools
- **Synthesia** - AI avatar with script
- **Descript** - Easy editing with AI voiceover
- **Lumen5** - Turn script into video
- **Pictory** - Script to video with stock footage
- **HeyGen** - Realistic AI presenters
