## Problem 2: "I Always Overspend"

You tell yourself you'll spend less on food this month. Or entertainment. Or shopping. But without a system, willpower alone doesn't work.

You want to save for that vacation. Or a new phone. Or just build an emergency fund. But somehow, saving always gets pushed to "next month."

SpendWise flips the script with goal-based saving. Create a savings goal, set your target amount and deadline, and SpendWise tells you exactly how much you need to save each month.

## The Unique Part: AI That Actually Helps

Here's where SpendWise is completely different from every other finance app.

Most apps just show you data. SpendWise actually thinks about your data.

Powered by Groq's LLaMA 3.3 - one of the world's most advanced AI models - SpendWise analyzes your income, expenses, budgets, and goals to give you personalized insights.

Your Financial Health Score tells you exactly how well you're managing money on a scale of 0 to 100. It's like a credit score, but for your spending habits.

But it doesn't stop there.

The AI gives you three types of insights based on YOUR actual data:

SUCCESS MESSAGES when you're doing well:
"Amazing! You saved 35% of your income this month - that's way above average."

WARNING MESSAGES when something needs attention:
"Heads up - your food spending is 40% higher than last month."

SMART TIPS tailored to your situation:
"Based on your patterns, setting up automatic savings on payday could help you save an extra 15%."

This isn't generic advice from a blog. This is AI that has analyzed YOUR finances and is speaking directly to YOU.

And every Monday morning? SpendWise sends you a personalized weekly summary. What went well, what needs work, and what you can do better.

It's like waking up to a message from a financial advisor who actually knows your situation.
